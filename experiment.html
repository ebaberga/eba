For example: - velocity= distance /time Distance and time are measureable factors, and velocity is the calculated. Common prefixes and scientific notation
  • Scientific notation: is a method of writing repeated zero in the form of 10 the power of positive integer. “b x 10 postive integer” the value of “b” should be between 0 and 10. Eg: 160000m= 1.6 x 105 m
  • Prefixes: are names given for a specific value of 10postive integer. Eg: 1018=exa if it is a distance then 1018 will be examerter.
